# Heat Shock Transformation

### Author
Opentrons

### Partner

## Categories
* Molecular Biology
	* Transformations

## Description
Perform a 1 minute heat shock transformation on six samples.

### Time Estimate
1 hour

### Robot
* OT PRO 
* OT Standard
* OT Hood

### Modules
* CoolDeck
* HeatDeck

### Reagents
* cold_deck[B1, B2, B3, B4, B5, B6] DNA samples
* cold_deck[A1, A2, A3, A4, A5, A6] Competent Cells
* tube_rack[A1] LB

## Process
1. Add DNA to competent cells.
2. Incubate on ice for 30 minutes.
3. Heat shock cells/DNA for 1 minute.
4. Incubate on ice for 5 minutes.
5. Add LB to cells/DNA.
